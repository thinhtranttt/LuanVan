Candy Heaven Pack 2.0

This extremely affordable and delicious package contains 53 classic candies! From lollipops to chocolate bars, everything you need to make your game irresistible.

The models are between 38 and 208 polygons. The package comes with 256x256, 512x512 and 1024x1024 textures - diffuse (Unity 4), albedo (Unity 5) and normal maps. There are also prefabs for all the different texture resolutions to make life easier for you. All the models are available as FBX files. 

We plan on adding to this package in the future and it's price will go up, but everyone who already owns the package will of course get all updates for free.
